ADPi

I joined Alpha Delta Pi in March of my freshman year. I never thought I would join a sorority but it was the best decision I've ever made. I've made lifelong friends and moved into leadership roles which have helped me grow. I've been able to engage in philanthropic opportunities and give back to my community. I'll never forget these girls and I love them all to death.

FSC Cheerleading

I have been on the FSC Cheerleading team for 3 years now! We cheer for men's and women's basketball games from October to March. We also appear at freshman admissions events and sponsership events to represent the college. I'm one of four flyers on the team which means I get thrown in the air which is so much fun! I'm hoping to be captain next year as well.
